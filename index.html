Welcome to Green Light

Our driving school stands apart from larger institutions by putting students at the center of everything we do. We understand that becoming a confident driver requires more than just technical skills - it demands personalized attention and support.

Rather than treating you as just another number, we create a nurturing environment where we help you overcome driving anxiety and build confidence behind the wheel. Our dedicated approach ensures every student receives the individualized care needed to succeed, transforming fear into competence through patient guidance and understanding.

About Us

Dick - Instructor and Owner

Since Richard or Dick, as he prefers to be called by his friends, arrived as a refugee with his family in the Netherlands in 2019, he has transformed his journey into a remarkable success story. His establishment of Green Light driving school represents not just personal achievement, but a commitment to helping others navigate their own path.

As Dick explains, his motivation stems from two powerful drivers: "To be able to give something back, but especially because this is a great opportunity for newcomers." Through his business, he actively seeks to support students from diverse backgrounds, creating a positive cycle of opportunity and integration.
